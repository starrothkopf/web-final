The poem will resemble you

On the emptiness of language and the fullness that can be interpreted into it.

Writers and artists from many eras have used randomness to resist to traditional methods of meaning-making, to uncover the unconscious, or to reject the value of utility all together. But to a computer scientist, randomness is an essential tool. Unpredictable algorithms allow statisticians to sample without bias, security specialists to encrypt, and game designers to make roguelikes replayable.


This website offers examples of randomness as a site of unity between the creative and the technical, using uselessness to release the algorithm from practicality and into the functions of lyric and myth.
